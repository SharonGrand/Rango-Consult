About Rango Consult

Rango Consult is a multidisciplinary consulting firm offering integrated advisory solutions in audit, risk management, finance, governance, and technology. We support organizations in building resilient, compliant, and scalable operations.

Our History

Established in 2009 and later incorporated in 2022, Rango Consult has evolved into a trusted one-stop professional services partner. Over the years, we have grown our expertise across multiple disciplines to meet the changing needs of businesses in a dynamic environment.

Who We Are

We are a team of professionals combining industry knowledge, technical expertise, and practical insight to deliver measurable results. Our approach is built on integrity, excellence, and a commitment to long-term client success.
